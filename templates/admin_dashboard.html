Upload Student Results

Welcome to the Upload Results section. Here, you can upload student semester results in CSV or Manual format. Click the button below to open the upload page.

Before uploading, please make sure:

  • All student data is accurate and matches the registered records.
  • The file format is either CSV or Excel.
  • The columns follow this structure:
    • Register Number
    • Student Name
    • Semester
    • Subject Code, Subject Name, Credits, Grade, Attendance % for each subject
  • Each record should be complete; incomplete data may cause errors in reports.
  • Once uploaded, results can be reviewed and corrected if necessary.
Go to Upload

Manage Student Records

The Manage Students section allows you to view, edit or delete student records. All student details such as registration number, name, email, department, batch and personal information can be managed here.

  • Add New Student: Fill in all required fields including name, register number, email, password, department, batch, DOB and gender.
  • Edit Student: Update student information including name, email, department, batch, DOB and gender.
  • Delete Student: Remove a student record permanently from the system.
  • Search & Filter: Quickly find students by name, registration number, department or batch.

Make sure to enter accurate information while adding or updating student records. Inaccurate data may affect reports, attendance tracking and result management.

Go to Manage Students

Manage Student Attendance

The Attendance section allows you to track and manage student attendance records. You can upload attendance data for each semester and subject.

  • Register Number: Enter the unique student ID for each student.
  • Semester: Specify the semester for which attendance is being uploaded.
  • Subject: Enter the subject code or name.
  • Total Classes: Total number of classes conducted.
  • Attended Classes: Number of classes attended by the student.

Accurate attendance data is important for calculating attendance percentages, issuing warnings, and generating reports. Ensure that each entry is verified before submission.

Go to Manage Attendance

Student Queries

The Queries section allows you to view and respond to questions or requests submitted by students. Queries can relate to profile updates, attendance, results, or other academic concerns.

  • View Queries: See all pending and resolved queries submitted by students.
  • Reply to Queries: Respond to student questions directly through the system. Pending queries will have a reply form.
  • Edit Profile Requests: For queries related to profile updates, you can navigate to the student profile to make corrections.
  • Status Tracking: Track which queries are Pending or Replied.

Make sure to respond to pending queries promptly. Accurate and timely replies improve student satisfaction and maintain the integrity of student records.

Go to View Queries

Reports & Analytics

The Reports section provides comprehensive summaries of student performance and attendance. Administrators can view overall statistics, download reports and analyze academic trends.

  • Total Students: Displays the total number of students enrolled.
  • Total Queries: Shows the count of all student queries submitted.
  • Total Departments: Displays the number of different departments.
  • Downloadable Reports: Generate CSV or TXT reports for analysis.
  • Analytics: Review department-wise results and attendance summaries.

Regularly reviewing reports ensures data accuracy and early identification of students needing additional support.

Go to View Reports
