Student Queries

The Queries section allows you to view and respond to questions or requests submitted by students. Queries can relate to profile updates, attendance, results or other academic concerns.

  • View Queries: See all pending and resolved queries submitted by students.
  • Reply to Queries: Respond to student questions directly through the system. Pending queries will have a reply form.
  • Edit Profile Requests: For queries related to profile updates, you can navigate to the student profile to make corrections.
  • Status Tracking: Track which queries are Pending or Replied.

Make sure to respond to pending queries promptly. Accurate and timely replies improve student satisfaction and maintain the integrity of student records.
